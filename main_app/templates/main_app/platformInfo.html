For Front Line Workers

You already are going out of your way to save other people's lives. On top of that, keeping your family and community safe must also be your priority. So finding a place to socially distance yourself should not be another hassle for you.

That is why we bring you 'Soteria'. Just like the name, Soteria, which signified the goddess of safety in the greek mythology, we are here to bring safety to you. Our easy to use platform provides the places you can rent out during the pandemic.

From our platform, you can search for the locations closest and most convenient to you. You can also have a look at the images of the place. On top of that, you can personally get in touch with the Landlord.

For Landlords

Follow your civic duty and provide your empty space for a front line worker. In these uncertain times, your mortgage will be taken care of.
